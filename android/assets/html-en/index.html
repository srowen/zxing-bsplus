This version removes or disables most functions that require special permissions, including: send history, send barcode, scan from file, configure wi-fi, scan NFC tags, and vibration.

Barcode Scanner+ Simple uses the camera on your device to read barcodes of all kinds. After scanning barcodes you find on products, for example, you can discover prices and reviews for it. Or, when scanning newer two-dimensional barcodes, like QR Codes, you can access contact information, bookmarks, web site links and more inside.
